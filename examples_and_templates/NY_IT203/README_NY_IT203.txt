Open Tax Solveer - NY State Tax Returns
---------------------------------------

In here are included programs, templates, and examples for
New York State tax form IT-203.

The example*.txt file is included for testing.
The template*.txt file is a blank starting form for entering
your tax data.

Compile:   
  cc taxsolve_NY_IT203_2014.c -o taxsolve_NY_IT203_2014

Run:
  ./taxsolve_NY_IT203_2014  NY_IT203_2014_template.txt

To download the pdf forms and fill them out run the following,
  ../../bin/fill_form_NY_IT203_2014 NY_IT203_2014_out.txt
